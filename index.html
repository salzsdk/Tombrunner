Game Features

⛰️ Mythical Island Legends

Journey through an uncharted Pacific paradise deeply rooted in ancient lore. The vibrant sky, shifting tides, and monolithic stone guardians aren't just a backdrop—they are part of a living world watching your every move.

💀 Deadly Ancient Trials

The paths to the hidden tombs are heavily guarded. Navigate crumbling ruins packed with razor-sharp wooden spikes, unstable falling platforms, and hidden traps designed to crush intruders.

⚡ Razor-Sharp Controls

Built for fans of challenging, high-stakes 2D platformers. The game features physics-tuned movement, responsive jump mechanics, and precise collision detection where timing is everything.
